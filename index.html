The ‘People’s Flag for Birmingham

Brummies are being invited to take pride in their city and wrap themselves in the flag, but they will have to design one first of all.

The Birmingham the civic flag belongs to the council “and no one is allowed to fly it without the council’s permission”, so Labour councillor Mike Leddy is welcoming submissions for a flag designed by the people, for the people.

Chamberlain Square Timelapse

Continuing our recent trend with time-lapse videos, here is one of Chamberlain Square slowly evolving into the soon-to-be-demolished central library.

In the 1960s and 70s research chemist and amateur photographer Derek Fairbrother made over 20 photographic timelapse sequences showing the demolition of old buildings and their replacement by new buildings and road systems in Birmingham city centre.

Birmingham Open Code Have a New Venue

Birmingham Open Code, the social study meetup group run by Jessica Rose, have relocated from The Woodman to their new home at Birmingham Open Media (BOM).

Every Tuesday evening, from 6-till-9pm, anyone looking for peer support in learning new skills, are invited to bring their laptop and get involved.
