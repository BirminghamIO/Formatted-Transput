Removing the "ham" from Birmingham

You must have been living under a rock to have missed this one, but according to Fox News, Birmingham is "totally Muslim where non-Muslims just simply don't go in".

It didn't take long for the ridiculousness of this statement to be parodied, as the Twitter hash-tag #foxnewsfacts filled with some of the best examples of British humour.

Following an apology by the "complete idiot", the BBC published an article with Nine real facts about Birmingham.

A city with no memory

In a recent a public meeting held to discuss the impact of the City Council’s proposed budget cuts on Library of Birmingham Dr Chris Callow, a historian based the University of Birmingham, suggested that the potential reduction of staff, expertise and opening hours to the city’s archives would leave Birmingham as a "city with no memory".

Architects' plans to transform Centenary Square revealed

With the redevelopment of Paradise Circus well underway, the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA) and the Landscape Institute have released five sets of proposals that would drastically alter Centenary Square.

The CGIs are part of a competition for the public space that is being launched on behalf of Birmingham City Council.
