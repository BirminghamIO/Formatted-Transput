Fifteen things to look forward to this year!
Birmingham is booming, and 2015 promises to be one of the most significant years in the city’s proud history.

A combination of showpiece landmark redevelopment, big sporting events and anniversary celebrations mean there will be plenty of red letter days.

So here are 15 reasons to be cheerful in ‘15.

 
£600m Snow Hill Masterplan to transform Birmingham business district
An ambitious £600 million masterplan aiming to help create “Birmingham’s Canary Wharf” has been launched.

The 20-year Snow Hill Masterplan, which covers the Snow Hill and Colmore areas of the city, has been launched as part of plans to make the city a global finance hub.

 
Lego competition returns to Birmingham
This January, the University of Birmingham hosted the first LEGO League for the 2nd year running, which saw the Munrow Sports hall filled with over 130 master builders of all ages.

Boys and girls alike attended the West Midlands heats; budding engineers at heart with their robotic LEGO creations in hand, with Lego Movie Emmet finding his way onto the walls of Moor Street station.
